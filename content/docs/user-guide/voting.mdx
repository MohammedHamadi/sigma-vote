# How to Vote

1. **Login:** Make sure you are authenticated.
2. **Select Election:** Navigate to the "Active Elections" page and choose an open election.
3. **Register:** If you haven't already, click the "Register to Vote" button. Your browser will perform the blind signature process.
4. **Cast Vote:** Select your preferred candidate and click "Submit". Wait a few moments while your browser encrypts the ballot and generates the necessary zero-knowledge proofs.
5. **Confirmation:** You will receive a transaction hash confirming your vote was recorded.
